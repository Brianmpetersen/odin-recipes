Banana Bread

Description

Ingredients:
  • 5 bananas (ripe)
  • 100g melted butter
  • 250g flour
  • 250g sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs

Steps:

  1. Set oven to 175 degrees (c)
  2. Whip eggs and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add butter - whip more!
  4. Mash bananas with a fork. Add to the batter.
  5. Mix flour, vanilla and baking soda in a bowl.
  6. Combine the dry parts slowly into the wets while stirring.
