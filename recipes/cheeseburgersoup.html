Cheeseburger Soup

Description

Ingredients
  • 150g bacon
  • 500g minced beef
  • 50g onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 500g cabbage
  • 2tsp spidskommen?
  • 1tsp cayenne pepper
  • 8dl beef boullion
  • 40g tomato puré
  • 100g cheddar
  • 200g creme cheese
  • butter for frying
Condiment
  • 100g cheddar

Steps

  1. Slice bacon into dices and fry. Take out the bacon but leave the grease.
  2. Fry the beef in the bacon fat til well done.
  3. Chop onion and garlic fine. Then chop cabbage into tiny parts - use a food processor if possible.
  4. Melt butter in a large pot. Fry onion and garlic till onion is transparent.
  5. Add the fried beef including the grease and add the cabbage.
  6. Add herbs, boullion, tomato puré, cheddar and creme cheese.
  7. Mix well and leave to cook for a bit.
  8. lower the heat and let it simmer for 30 mins.
  9. Taste with salt and pepper.
  10. Serve with bacon dices and cheddar on top
  11. Enjoy!
